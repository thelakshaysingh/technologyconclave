Registration

Secure your place at India’s flagship engineering conclave.

Register as a delegate, paper presenter, poster contributor, or start-up exhibitor. Selected extended abstracts will be considered for publication in Transactions of INAE (Springer Nature).

Registration includes participation across both days, conclave kit, lunches, conclave dinner, cultural program, and the proceedings booklet. Awards for Best Abstract, Best Poster and Best Start-Up Display (plus Runners-Up) will be conferred at the Valedictory Session.
