The Technology Conclave is INAE's flagship event, strengthening Industry-Academia-Government collaboration to drive technological innovation and self-reliance under Atmanirbhar Bharat.

The 2026 edition will gather engineering researchers, faculty and professionals for technical discourse, contributory abstract presentations, posters, and an industry & start-up exposition - anchored by plenary lectures and panel discussions from India's most distinguished engineers.

Founded on 20 April 1987 as a Registered Society under the Societies Registration Act of 1860 and headquartered at Technology Bhavan, New Delhi, the Indian National Academy of Engineering comprises India’s most distinguished engineers, engineer-scientists and technologists covering the entire spectrum of engineering disciplines. INAE represents India at the International Council of Academies of Engineering and Technological Sciences (CAETS).
