Submission Guidelines
  • Extended Abstracts: up to 4 pages, single-spaced, justified, 12 pt Times New Roman. Selected abstracts will be considered for publication in Transactions of INAE (Springer Nature).
  • Posters: maximum size 4' x 6'. Suggested headings: Introduction / Abstract, Problem Statement, System Architecture, Implementation, Outcomes, Conclusion. Minimum font size 40 pt.
  • Start-Up Displays: exhibit innovations & demos at the conclave. Selected start-ups receive special commendation and awards.
  • Deadline: 30 June 2026. Notification: 5 July 2026.
  • Queries: technologyconclave@inae.in

The Technology Conclave is INAE's flagship event, strengthening Industry-Academia-Government collaboration for a self-reliant, technologically advanced India.
